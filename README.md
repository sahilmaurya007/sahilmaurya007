## 🧑‍🔬 About Me

**Research Scholar** at the Indian Institute of Technology Mandi (School of Computing & Electrical Engineering — **A²DSL Lab**).  

I specialize in **Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC) hardware**, **Digital VLSI Design**, and **RISC-V SoC development**. My focus is on building secure, high-performance accelerators — particularly RLWE-based modular multipliers and NTT units for NIST standards (Kyber/ML-KEM, Dilithium/ML-DSA) — using FPGA prototyping (Vivado) and open-source ASIC flows (OpenLane/Sky130), with strong emphasis on Power-Performance-Area (PPA) optimization and side-channel resistance.
