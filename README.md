👨‍🔬 About Me
I am a Research Scholar in the School of Computing and Electrical Engineering (SCEE) at the Indian Institute of Technology Mandi (IIT Mandi). My research lies at the exciting intersection of hardware security, cryptographic hardware design, and modern processor architecture.
My work focuses on building secure, efficient, and high-performance hardware systems — from cryptographic co-processors resilient to quantum attacks, to full RISC-V SoC implementations on FPGA and ASIC platforms.
---
🔬 Research Interests
Domain	Focus Areas
🔐 Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC)	Hardware implementation of NIST PQC standards (CRYSTALS-Kyber, Dilithium, SPHINCS+), side-channel resistant architectures
⚙️ Digital VLSI Design	RTL design, ASIC synthesis & physical design, low-power techniques, timing closure, DFT
🖥️ RISC-V SoC Development	Custom RISC-V core design, SoC integration, peripheral IP development, FPGA prototyping
