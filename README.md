## 🧑‍🔬 About Me

I am a **Research Scholar** at the Indian Institute of Technology Mandi (SCEE Department) working in the **A²DSL Lab**. My research focuses on building **secure, high-performance hardware** at the intersection of:

- **Digital VLSI Design** (RTL-to-GDSII flows, synthesis, STA, low-power techniques)
- **Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC)** — Hardware acceleration of NIST standards (CRYSTALS-Kyber/ML-KEM, CRYSTALS-Dilithium/ML-DSA) using RLWE, NTT, and side-channel resistant architectures
- **RISC-V SoC Development** — Custom processor extensions with AXI4/APB interconnects and integrated cryptographic IPs

I have hands-on experience with **FPGA prototyping (Vivado)** and **open-source ASIC flows (OpenLane / Sky130 PDK)**, with a strong emphasis on measurable PPA metrics (Power-Performance-Area), timing closure, and real-world security.

*"Secure hardware is not a feature — it is a foundation for the post-quantum era."*
