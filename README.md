## 🧑‍🔬 About Me

**Research Scholar** at the Indian Institute of Technology Mandi (School of Computing & Electrical Engineering — **A²DSL Lab**).  

I specialize in **secure hardware design** for the post-quantum era, focusing on:

- **Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC)** hardware acceleration (RLWE-based modular multipliers, NTT units for Kyber/Dilithium)
- **Digital VLSI Design** (RTL-to-GDSII flows, synthesis, STA, low-power optimization, PPA metrics)
- **RISC-V SoC Development** with custom cryptographic extensions and neuromorphic adaptations

My work bridges FPGA prototyping (Vivado) and open-source ASIC implementation (OpenLane/Sky130), with published research on approximate modular multipliers for R-LWE cryptosystems.
